How Our Smart Matching System Works:

  • Resource Matching: Volunteers are matched based on the type of resources required, such as food, medical supplies, or shelter.
  • Distance Optimization: Volunteers located closest to victims are given priority for faster assistance.
  • Urgency Prioritization: Victims with high urgency are matched first, ensuring the most critical cases are addressed immediately.
  • Feedback Loop: Volunteers and victims can provide feedback to improve our system's effectiveness.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Our team continuously monitors the situation to ensure proper coordination and prevent errors.
